Context:

As team our mission was must improve waste management in rural MacMasterton by developing a system that eliminates environmental and health hazards while creating value. The solution should balance economic and environmental concerns and integrate with current operations. Consider key problems, regulations, industrial capacities, resources, space constraints, and community impacts. Be prepared to present and address any pushback.

Summary:

  • We decided to focus on solving the waste water issue in MacMasterton
  • Resasoning: MacMasterton produces 3.10 megaliters of wastewater per day with no centralized treatment facility uses soil absorption, mound and septic tank systems, and onsite irrigation for treatment, which require regular maintenance and inspection.
  • Our solution of introducing the biobore piping system, with its smaller diameter, affordability, pressurized water flow, and ability to reduce total contaminants discharged by 26%, was the optimal solution for MacMasterton
