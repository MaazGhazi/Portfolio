OSTO+

Date: September 2022

Skills:

  • CAD
  • Teamwork
  • Engineering Design

Overview:

Our patient Lieko, is an ostomate and has no time in her day so she finds herself eating at night. This causes her to excrete a larger quantity of stool than her bag can handle while she is sleeping. However, her current ostomy bag cannot handle the quantity of stool so she finds her self waking up to empty it resulting in disturbed sleep. That's why my team and I bring forth Osto+

Key Takeaways

  • Created aluminum connector piece that funnels the waste the comes out of the stoma into Osto+ bag
  • Worked with a team of 4 to collectively to create a bag that will periodically increase the volume by 50%
  • Pitched idea similar to shark to style in the 3MT showcase
